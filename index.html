My Favourite Hobby
Reading books

HI!

My hobby is reading interesting, suspensful as well as fantasy books. My favourite books are the Harry Potter Series, Percy Jackson Series, With Love From The Hills ~ Ruskin Bond, Malgudi Days, 5 Mystery Stories, Sherlock Holmes, Arabian Nights, Sindbad The Sailor. Reading books helps me in improving my vocabulary and it is also a great way to spend time. My favourite time to read books are when its raining and when there is sun during the winter season. Otherwise, I read books while I sit on my couch. This also helps me improve my imagination. This has helped me when I write stories, when I speak in fornt of many people, when there is a random topic given and you have to speak on it.
